PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated November 20, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share (‘process’) your personal information when you use our Services by downloading and using our mobile application SafePass.

If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We receive all the information you create in the mobile application when you use it. We may also collect the information you voluntarily provide us to contact you and to provide you with important information.

If you use our mobile application, we also may collect some device information (such as your mobile device ID, model), operating system, and version information.

We do not collect any information from third parties.

We do not process any sensitive information.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS THE INFORMATION COLLECTED?

We process the information collected to provide and administer our Services and/or make the mobile application function, and to diagnose and fix technical issues.

We process your information only when we have a valid legal reason to do so.

3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE INFORMATION COLLECTED?

We may share your data with third-party service providers who help us improve our Services, for example, Google Play Services, Google Analytics for Firebase, and Firebase Crashlytics.

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to these third parties.

Please also note that we utilize third-party services that have their own privacy policies about handling data.

We also may disclose the information received when it’s required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, or to protect our/your rights.

4. HOW DO WE KEEP THE INFORMATION SAFE?

We are committed to ensuring the security of information. All information is stored locally on your device, and we do not store or transfer any information to our servers.

We use encryption and other security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. WHAT ARE YOUR RIGHTS?

You can stop the mobile application from collecting information by simply uninstalling it. You can use standard uninstallation processes that may be available on your mobile device or through the mobile application marketplace or network.

We do not and will never sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to an unrelated third party for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

6. UPDATES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY?

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

7. HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US?

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the mobile application, or have questions about the practices, please contact us via email at customersupport@cash-express.ph.
